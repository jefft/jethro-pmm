# Jethro PMM

**Pastoral Ministry Manager** — Jethro is church management software that helps you care for your congregation, track involvement, and communicate effectively.

![Overview](/img/jethro.png)

## What Jethro Does

Jethro brings together everything you need to manage a church community:

- **People & Families** — Track individuals and households with contact details, demographics, and pastoral notes
- **Groups & Attendance** — Organise small groups, ministry teams, and committees; record who attended
- **Rosters & Services** — Schedule volunteers and plan church services
- **Notes** — Keep a pastoral communication log linked to each person
- **SMS Communication** — Send personalised or bulk text messages with delivery tracking
- **Documents** — Generate letters, certificates, and mail merges from templates
- **Member Portal** — Let church members update their own details and view rosters
- **Public Area** — Share service times and rosters publicly


### At a Glance

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **People records** | Name, contact, age bracket, congregation, status — everything about a person in one place |
| **Family grouping** | Households with shared address and phone |
| **Pastoral notes** | Timeline of every interaction: phone calls, visits, emails |
| **Group management** | Small groups, ministry teams, committees with member roles |
| **Attendance** | Track who was present at groups and services |
| **Rosters** | Volunteer scheduling with recurring assignments |
| **Services** | Plan the order of service with songs, readings, and roles |
| **SMS** | Send individual or bulk messages, schedule for later, track delivery |
| **Documents** | DOCX/ODT templates with merge fields for personalised letters |
| **Permissions** | Role-based access with congregation-level scoping |
